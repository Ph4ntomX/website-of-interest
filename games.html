Kanto Games

There have multiple games where Kanto is featured, both as a main region and side region.

Some notable games include the original generation 1 games, and even the generation 2 games which have Kanto as a region you can visit after you beat the Pokemon League.

Generation 1

Pokemon Red, Blue, Yellow

Pokemon Red, Blue, and Yellow are the first installments of the Pokémon video game series, and were first released in Japan as Pocket Monsters Red and Pocket Monsters Green, followed by the special edition Pocket Monsters Blue later that year. An enhanced version named Pokémon Yellow Version: Special Pikachu Edition, was released in Japan in 1998 and in other regions in 1999 and 2000.

Release date
