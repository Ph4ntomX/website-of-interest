About Kanto

Kanto was the first region introduced in the Pokémon world, making its debut in 1996.

This eastern region draws inspiration from the real-life Kantō region of Japan.

Kanto has been featured in various Pokémon games and media.

Kanto Gyms and Cities

Pewter City - Brock

Pewter City sits at the foot of a great stone mountain.

Here you can find the Pewter Museum of Science, which has many Fossils on display.

Brock, the Pewter City Gym Leader, specializes in Rock-type Pokémon.

Cerulean City - Misty

Cerulean City is known for its beautiful water features.

Here you can find the Bike Shop, which can make your travels easier in the games.

Misty, the Cerulean City Gym Leader, is a master of Water-type Pokémon.

Vermilion City - Lt. Surge

Vermilion City is a bustling port city.

This port city is home to the S.S. Anne, a luxurious cruise ship that only docks here once.

It is also home to the Electric-type Gym Leader, Lt. Surge.

Celadon City - Erika

Celadon City is known for its large department store.

One of the largest cities in Kanto, Celadon is secretly home to a Team Rocket hideout.

Erika, the Gym Leader, specializes in Grass-type Pokémon.

Fuchsia City - Koga

Fuchsia City is home to the Safari Zone.

This city also has the Pokémon Zoo, where trainers can view rare Pokémon up close before exploring the Safari Zone.

Koga, the Gym Leader, is a master of Poison-type Pokémon.

Saffron City - Sabrina

Saffron City is the largest city in Kanto.

A major city in the center of Kanto. It contains the Silph Co. building, targeted by Team Rocket for its technology.

Sabrina, the Gym Leader, specializes in Psychic-type Pokémon.

Cinnabar Island - Blaine

Cinnabar Island is known for its volcanic activity.

This city has the Pokémon Lab, where scientists revived ancient Pokémon from fossils.

Blaine, the Gym Leader, is a master of Fire-type Pokémon.

Viridian City - Giovanni

Viridian City - ???

Viridian City is a quiet town with a mysterious Gym.

This city houses the final Gym in the Kanto League.

Giovanni, the Gym Leader, specializes in Ground-type Pokémon.
